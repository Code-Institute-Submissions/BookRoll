About

My name is Daphne, the founder and owner of BookRoll. I have over 20 years of Finance and Admin experience working with a range of businesses from construction firms to leisure clubs, international dance companies and most recently for one of the most recognised names in the Northern Ireland retail sector.

BookRoll is a highly cost effective bookkeeping and payroll solution for your business. Outsourcing with us not only takes the headache off your hands but also gives you more time to concentrate on growing and developing your business. We have a passion for developing great relationships with our clients. If you feel like we would be a great fit for your business the please get in touch.
